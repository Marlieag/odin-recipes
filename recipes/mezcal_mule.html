Pineapple Mezcal Mule

Here is another recipe that I love from Chicano eats. Now I am a big fan of Moscow Mules, traditionally these drinks are made with vodka and lime, but in this recipe we will be using mezcal instead of vodka. Now if you are not familiar with mezcal it is similar to tequila, but only in the sense that they are both made from an agave plant. Fun fact: tequila can only be made from one type of agave plant, the Blue Weber, on the other hand mezcal from a variety of agave plants. The smoky flavor of mezcal goes well with the ingredients below. I hope you enjoy this cocktail as much as I did.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 1/2 oz mezcal
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 can of ginger beer (typically come in 6 oz)

Instructions

  1. In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, pour in the pineapple juice, mezcal, and fresh lime juice. Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds, the pour the mixture into a highball glass filled with ice. Top with your favorite ginger beer.
  2. OPTIONAL: Garnish with pineapple leaves or chunks
